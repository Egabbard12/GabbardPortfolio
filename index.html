Project Background

Traffic Jam Assist is a system based on Adaptive Cruise Control. When using Adaptive Cruise Control your vehicle will continue at a set speed unless it detects a vehicle in front of yours. Then the system will adjust according to the speed of the car ahead of you until it senses no more obstacles.

Project Description

Traffic Jam Assist hopes to build on this. On particular approved highways the system hopes to allow adjustment to traffic flow up to and including stopping completely until the flow of traffic is no longer obstructed. Then the system will continue on the pre-set speed from before you stopped.
